One of the pillars on which a successful event stands is-“ Sponsorship”. Formally speaking, Sponsorship is a form of affinity marketing that provides certain rights and benefits to the buyer or “sponsor” which is usually in conjunction with a property, venue, personality, or event. It touts a broad spectrum of benefits for the sponsors such as helping to develop closer and better relationships with customers, brand visibility, community goodwill, showcasing opportunities of services and products etc. On the other hand, it also benefits the organizers as the sponsors provide a financial partnership for the smooth conduction of events and also attracts a large pool of audiences and their participation which leads the event into success. The entire procedure of Sponsorship is taken care of by the Sponsorship Co-Ordinators.
